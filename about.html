About WilderBliss

WilderBliss began
with a question.

What would it look like to step away — properly?

The Story

Not a hotel. Not a retreat.
Something quieter.

The answer turned out to be land. Specifically: working farmland in the south-east of Ireland, and the belief that it could hold something valuable if treated with enough care.

Stay by WilderBliss is the first expression of that — a private off-grid cabin placed quietly within a working farm, designed for two people who want to stop for a while.

Breathe and Gather follow the same logic. Different experiences, the same intention: to create space that feels worth slowing down for.

The Philosophy

Rarity & contrast.

WilderBliss is defined by contrast.

  • Alone, without isolation.
  • Luxury, without indulgence.
  • Discovery, without effort.
  • Sustainability, without signalling.

The experience is intentionally non-performative. There is no reception desk, no programmed activities, no curated spectacle. Guests arrive, settle, and exist on their own terms.

The Land

Built on partnership.

WilderBliss is built on partnership with the land and the people who tend it. A portion of every stay supports the landowners who host our cabins. We don't reshape the landscape — we work within it.

Our cabins are placed on working land through respectful partnership. They sit lightly. They preserve the character of the landscape. They do not reshape it.

We embrace the land as it is.
